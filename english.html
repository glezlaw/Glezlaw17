My Services

I provide comprehensive advisory services tailored to the unique needs of expatriates and international businesses establishing themselves in Spain. Each service is designed to ensure a smooth transition and successful integration into the Spanish environment.

Residency & Fiscal Planning

Guidance on Spanish residency permits, NIE applications, and strategic tax planning including the Beckham Law regime for qualifying professionals.

Tax Optimization

Strategic tax planning for expatriates, including analysis of applicable tax regimes and optimization of cross-border income and investments.

Business Advisory

Support for international businesses and entrepreneurs establishing operations in Spain, including company structuring and compliance guidance.

Property Acquisition Support

Assistance with the property acquisition process in Spain, including due diligence review and coordination with local legal professionals.

Comprehensive Relocation Support

End-to-end support for individuals and families relocating to Spain, from administrative procedures to cultural integration guidance.

Cross-Border Succession Planning

Advisory on international inheritance matters and succession planning for assets located in multiple jurisdictions.
